Meet Hannah

Hannah’s Hat is a lovely story in rhyme that tells the journey of a little girl and a school assignment. Hannah travels through self-doubt, worry, and other insecurities along the way and comes out the other side a very happy, and self-assured girl with a hat, a feather, and a special ribbon.

Hannah's Hat was conceived by author Libby Berke in YEAR. When she read her friend, Jenn Mason's, bucket list online which included "illustrate a childrens' book", she knew she found a partner-in-crime to bring her story to life.

Illustrations
The illustrations are hand created with acrylic paint, pens, found paper, and bits of whimsy to create an engaging and three-dimensional look.

Libby Kavanagh Berke

Libby Kavanagh wrote Hannah’s Hat in 2000 after her daughter Hannah was born. Since that time she became a mom again to Julia, and has worked for a software company, a daycare center, a repair shop, a public health organization, and a venture philanthropy fund--and all that time has dreamed of publishing this story. Happily, she found the perfect illustrator in her Brookline, MA neighbor Jenn Mason.

Jenn Mason

Jenn Mason is a creative entrepreneur who finds herself at times working on startups, curating content, writing books, hosting television, consulting, making art, and now illustrating childrens’ books--a long time goal realized happily! She does all this from her home studio in Brookline, MA.
